Privacy Policy for Silent Library Website

Last Updated: 28 Mar 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Silent Library's website. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit our website.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, library card number, and other details you provide when registering for an account, requesting services, or contacting us.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our website (e.g., pages visited, time spent, IP address, browser type) through cookies and analytics tools.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide library services (e.g., account management, book reservations, event registrations).
  • Improve our website and user experience.
  • Communicate with you about library updates, events, or overdue items.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

4. Data Sharing & Security

  • We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.
  • We may share data with trusted service providers (e.g., library software vendors) only for operational purposes.
  • We implement security measures to protect your data but cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Cookies & Tracking

Our website may use cookies to enhance functionality. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work properly.

6. Your Rights

You may:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information by logging into your account or contacting us.
  • Opt out of promotional communications.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
